# Why document everything?

People often ask why so much effort is invested in documentation.

The answer is simple.

Because memory is unreliable.

Documentation preserves engineering knowledge.

A project without documentation slowly loses its history.

Eventually nobody remembers why certain decisions were made.

Architectures become mysterious.

Changes become risky.

Knowledge disappears.

I never wanted CHOMS Platform to suffer that fate.

Every important decision deserves an explanation.

Every phase deserves a conclusion.

Every architectural evolution deserves to be recorded.

Future contributors—including my future self—should always understand the reasoning behind the platform.
